Explanation:

Resize your browser window to see the changes:

  • Small screens: You'll see "MyLogo" and a hamburger icon. The full navigation links ("Home", "About", etc.) are hidden.
  • Medium screens and up (typically 768px and wider): The hamburger icon disappears, and the full navigation links become visible next to "MyLogo".

Gotcha Reminder: Clicking the hamburger icon does not open a menu in this example. Tailwind manages the display of elements based on screen size, but JavaScript is needed for click-based interactions like opening a mobile menu.

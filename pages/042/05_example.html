Explanation:

  • grid : Establishes a grid container.
  • grid-cols-1 : Default to 1 column on extra-small screens (mobile-first).
  • sm:grid-cols-2 : On small screens (640px and up), display 2 columns.
  • md:grid-cols-3 : On medium screens (768px and up), display 3 columns.
  • lg:grid-cols-4 : On large screens (1024px and up), display 4 columns.
  • gap-6 : Adds spacing between grid items.

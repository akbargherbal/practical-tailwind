Example 1: Basic Rightward Gradient

Hero Section with Gradient

Explanation:

The classes bg-gradient-to-r , from-purple-500 , and to-pink-500 work together.

  • bg-gradient-to-r : Sets the gradient direction to "right". Other directions include t (top), b (bottom), l (left), tr (top-right), bl (bottom-left), etc.
  • from-purple-500 : Sets the starting color of the gradient.
  • to-pink-500 : Sets the ending color of the gradient.

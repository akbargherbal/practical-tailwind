Button Corner Styles

Moderately Rounded: `rounded-md`

The `rounded-md` class applies a medium-sized border radius, giving a soft, modern look.

Pill-Shaped: `rounded-full`

The `rounded-full` class applies a large border radius, making the element pill-shaped (if its height is less than its width and padding is appropriate) or circular (if height and width are equal).

Note: For a perfect pill shape, horizontal padding is usually greater than vertical. For a circle, ensure equal height and width.
